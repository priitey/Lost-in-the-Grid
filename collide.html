collide

Design from a point of collision between the natural world and the artificial.

This statement is informed by my reading of Kerryn Higgs' Collision Course (2014).

It is evident that the limits of capitalism have long been met. Alternatives must be found.

Endless economic growth is not possible within our physical reality.

The dissemination and implementation of neoclassical economic theory throughout the various structures of cultures and societies has brought about the Myth of Growth.

Economics must be viewed from a pluralistic perspective. Environment and economy are not mutually exclusive. Economics doesn't exist in a vacuum; the environment shapes the economy as does the economy shape the environment.
