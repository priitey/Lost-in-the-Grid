iterate

The process is the product. Make tools that enable users to produce their own outputs.

This statement is informed by my learnings within creative coding and the Conditional Design manifesto by Luna Maurer, Edo Paulus, Jonathan Puckeu, and Roel Wouters.

In the time of me writing this, I have come to place significant value on the design of frameworks, methodolgies and tools.

Creativity exists within limitations, restrictions, rules. The formulation and engagement with designed circumstances is what I intend to practice.

Creative coding lends itself well to iteration. I have come to relish unexpected failures and novel experimentation that coding affords much opportunity for.

I accept that it is my responsibility to communicate a narrative from this sequence of logic and randomness. Ultimately, I want others to engage with the tools I design.
